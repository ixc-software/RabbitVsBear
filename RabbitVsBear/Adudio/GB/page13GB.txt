Hearing that, the bear's hair bristled up in back. And all his anger turned to the new rival who so unexpectedly appeared on his way. “What a useless nobody daring to enter my territory!” he roared, clawing ground. “Hey! rabbit, take me to him, I'll tear him to pieces.” “Noble sir,” the rabbit said, “he is a strong and terrible king.” “What? Do you think I'll be afraid of him? Take me to him right away and let's see who is stronger.” “Noble sir, but he is living in a stone castle.” “What do I care for his castle! Lead me to him, I'll get him even if he's hidden on the top of the highest tree!”
