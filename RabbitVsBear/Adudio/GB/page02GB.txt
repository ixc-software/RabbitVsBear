So the animals held a counsel and decided to send messengers to the bear to say, “Dear sir Bear, why do you torment us in such a way? You eat only one of us, but because of your anger hurt  ten of us. Indeed, in less than a year, there will not be a living soul in the forest. It would be better for you to sit calmly in your cave and we'll send you one of us every day so you can have a meal.”
