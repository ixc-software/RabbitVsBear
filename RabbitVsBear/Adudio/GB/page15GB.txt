The bear stood above the well and looked down - there really is a bear! “Do you see your enemy,” the rabbit said, “looking out of his fortress?” “I'll get him out of there, if it's the last thing I do!” the bear said and all of a sudden roared at the top of his lungs. And the roaring echoed from the well twice as loud as his, like from a huge tube. “So, that's what you're up to!” screamed the bear. “Are you threatening me? I'll show you who is better!”
