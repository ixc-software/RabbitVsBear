Hearing the bear's roar and furious words the hare began to tremble, but quickly pulled himself together and staying on his hind legs in front of the bear said as polite as possible, “Noble sir! It's not my fault that I've come so late. And you are not to blame the animals. Today, on your birth-day they gathered at dawn and sent four of us to you, and we all ran to you so that you, noble sir,  would have a good party.” “And what happened? Why have you come so late? And where are the other three?” the bear asked.
